# Neon-Retro-Futuristic Bowling Art Direction

## Current Direction
The visual style of this 3D bowling game blends retro elements of a classic 1980s bowling alley with a high-tech, futuristic cyberpunk neon world. The lane is made of a glossy, dark mahogany wood texture that is layered with glowing cyan/neon blue gridlines and neon pink borders. The surrounding atmosphere is a vast, dark retro-futuristic arcade space, featuring neon pillars, glowing synthwave grid skylines, and particle stars.

## Palette and Lighting
- **Dominant Colors**:
  - **Synthwave Violet & Purple**: Deep space backgrounds and general scene ambient glow.
  - **Neon Pink / Hot Magenta**: Lane borders, gutter indicators, strike celebrations, and secondary UI highlights.
  - **Cyan / Neon Blue**: Main lane grid lines, ball trails, interactive indicators, and primary UI highlights.
  - **Glossy Black / Dark Mahogany**: The bowling lane base wood, providing a solid contrast.
- **Lighting**:
  - **Ambient Light**: Muted dark purple ambient illumination to keep the shadows atmospheric and deep.
  - **Directional/Spotlight**: A bright cyan/pink spotlight pointing down the pin deck (pin area) to highlight the targets, casting realistic shadows.
  - **Emissive Materials**: Bowling pins, the bowling ball, and neon trails will have high emissive values to glow intensely in the dark space.

## Characters and Props
- **Bowling Ball**: A glowing, translucent glass-like obsidian sphere with pulsating neon core or wrapped in a glowing pink/blue cybernetic pattern. It emits a dynamic, pulsating blue-violet trail.
- **Bowling Pins**: Standard bowling pin silhouettes, but styled with a glowing glossy white material, featuring double-stripe neon-pink collars around their necks. When hit, they can spark with neon-blue particle effects.
- **The Lane**: Framed with neon tube lights along the gutters. The wood material has a highly reflective surface to bounce off the glows of the pins and the ball.

## UI and Icons
- **Typography**: Futurism styled using 'Orbitron' with neon glow styling (CSS text-shadow in cyan/magenta).
- **HUD**: Semi-transparent cybernetic dark panels with bright neon borders and cyber-grids. Includes a digital 3D pin state indicator (displaying which pins are still standing).
- **Animations**: Text pop-ups like "STRIKE!" or "SPARE!" zoom, flash, and vibrate with chromatic aberration and glowing effects.

## Asset Generation Notes
- Background: A retro synthwave horizon background, featuring a glowing wireframe grid terrain and neon sun. (Aspect ratio 16:9 for landscape display).
- Wood texture: A dark wood grain texture with clean horizontal and vertical mapping, used for the lane underlay.
- Glowing decals/icons for ball and UI.
